## Invitation Methods

SETTLERS has two ways to invite a player and make them your referral:

### 1. Clan Invitation
- Use the "Invite" button on the clan page
- Player joins the clan and automatically becomes your referral

### 2. Friend Invitation
- Use the "Invite friend" link from your profile
- Player registers as your referral
